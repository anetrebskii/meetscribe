Features
Everything you need from a meeting transcript
⚡
Real-time transcription
Captions are captured live as people speak — no delay, no post-processing.
👤
Speaker identification
Each line is automatically attributed to the right speaker by name.
📋
Meeting history
All past meetings are saved and searchable. Tap any meeting to review its full transcript.
📤
Markdown export
Export any transcript as a formatted Markdown file — ready to paste into Notion, Obsidian, or any notes app.
🌐
Multi-language support
Works with any language supported by Google Meet's live captions.
🔒
Fully private
All data stays on your device. No server, no account, no cloud sync.
How it works
Up and running in seconds
1
Install the extension
Add MeetScribe from the Chrome Web Store. No account or sign-up required.
2
Join a Google Meet call
MeetScribe starts capturing automatically as soon as captions are available.
3
Review your transcript
Click the MeetScribe icon after the call to read, search, or export the full transcript.
🔐

Your conversations stay on your device

MeetScribe never sends your transcript data anywhere. Everything is stored locally using Chrome's built-in storage API. Uninstalling the extension deletes all data permanently. Read the full privacy policy →
